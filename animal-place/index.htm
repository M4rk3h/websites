The Animal Place

Here at the animal place, we rescue exotic animals who have been mistreated and give them a second chance at life. We have specific environments for our vast range of animals. Why not pay us a visit located at the top of Cilfynydd Mountain.

Fundraise For Us

Due to being a non-profit charity, the animal place can really benefit from fundraising. All money made from fundraising goes directly to the animals, and we will spend it all on making the animals happier every day. From therapy to bigger environments for the animals.
