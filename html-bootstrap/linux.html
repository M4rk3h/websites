GNU/Linux

For the past couple of years I have had an interest in GNU/Linux, Here is a list of a bunch of popular new and old distributions listed below.

Arch Linux

A lightweight and flexible Linux distribution that tries to Keep It Simple.

- Desktop: It comes without one

- View Their Website

Chakra linux

An independent distro with strong ties to QT and the KDE desktop. It tries minimizing uses of other toolkits to stick with QT, and KDE.

- Desktop - KDE Plasma

- View Their Website

Debian

The Universal Operating System. Super stable, with most other distro's being based on this.

- Desktop(s): GNOME, KDE Plasma, XFCE, LXQT, and Mate

- View Their Website

Elementary OS

A fast and open replacement for Windows and macOS

- Desktop(s): It's own unique Pantheon desktop

- View Their Website

Fedora

Choose Freedom. Choose Fedora.

Desktop(s): GNOME, but has spins for Mate, Cinnamon, KDE Plasma, XFCE, and LXQT

- View Their Website

Kubuntu

The stable, and Ubuntu based KDE distro! Vanilla KDE Plasma combined with the stability and ease of use of Ubuntu!

- Desktop(s): The KDE Plasma Desktop

- View Their Website

Lubuntu

Lubuntu is a fast and lightweight operating system with a clean and easy-to-use user interface.

- Desktop (s): LXDE/LXQT

- View Their Website

Manjaro

Professional and user-friendly Linux at its best. Evolving and giving the Linux world a new face and operating experience.

- Desktop(s): XFCE, KDE Plasma, and GNOME, but has community editions for Budgie, Cinnamon, Deepin, Mate, and LXQT

- View Their Website

Mint

A modern, elegant and comfortable operating system which is both powerful and easy to use.

- Desktop(s): Mate, Cinnamon, and XFCE

- View Their Website

OpenSUSE

The newest Linux packages with OpenSUSE's rolling release. Fast! Integrated! Stabilized! Tested!

- Desktop(s): Cinnamon, Englightenment, Gnome, KDE Plasma, Mate, XFCE, and LXQT

- View Their Website

Pop!_OS

An OS for the software developer, maker, and computer science professional.

- Desktop(s): A customized GNOME desktop

- View Their Website

Tails

Privacy for anyone anywhere.

- Desktop(s): A customized GNOME desktop

- View Their Website

Ubuntu

Linux for Human Beings.

- Desktop(s): A customized GNOME desktop

Solus's home grown Budgie desktop, customizable yet quick, unique yet easy to use, now on Ubuntu!

Ubuntu MATE

Ubuntu Mate - For a retrospective future

Xubuntu

The lightweight yet powerful XFCE desktop, now on Ubuntu.
